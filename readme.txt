== Description ==
Adaptify SEO One-Click Publishing automates your SEO process, allowing you to rank higher on Google while you focus on your core business. 

**How does Adaptify SEO work?**
- **Website Analysis**: Adaptify analyzes your WordPress site to understand your business and customer profile.
- **SEO Strategy Creation**: It develops an SEO strategy with relevant keyword clusters tailored to your niche.
- **Automated Execution**: Adaptify automatically creates high-ranking content and publishes it directly to your WordPress site.
- **Optimization for Search Intent**: The content is optimized to address search intent, ensuring excellent Google rankings.

**AI-Powered Customization**
Adaptify uses advanced AI to gain deep insights into your business, crafting highly customized content that drives both traffic and conversions. Articles are interlinked automatically for optimal SEO performance, and custom images and infographics are generated to enhance the content.

**Thorough Content Research**
Each article is meticulously researched, utilizing the most reliable and up-to-date information to produce comprehensive content.

**Automated Backlink Building**
In addition to content creation, Adaptify builds backlinks through personalized outreach to reputable PR outlets, boosting your site's authority and ranking.



== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How do I configure the plugin? =
Setting up the plugin only takes a few clicks, go settings page in Adaptify for more instructions to connect Adaptify SEO with your website.

= Does this plugin work with other SEO plugins? =
Adaptify SEO is designed to complement your existing SEO plugins like Yoast, Rankmath, and AIOSEO, not replace them. It automates content creation and publication while working alongside these tools.

= How does Adaptify SEO improve my rankings? =
Adaptify created and publishes optimized content tailored to your business, targeting relevant keywords and addressing search intent, which improves your rankings on Google.

= What kind of content does Adaptify create? =
Adaptify creates high-quality articles, custom images, and infographics that are optimized for SEO and user engagement.

= Is there support available for setting up the plugin? =
Yes, our support team is available to help you with any setup or usage questions. You can reach out to us through our website or plugin support page.

= How often does Adaptify publish content? =
You can control the frequency of content publication in your Adaptify Account, choosing from manual, weekly, regular, or daily intervals.


= How does Adaptify handle backlinks? =
Adaptify automatically builds backlinks through personalized outreach to reputable PR outlets, enhancing your website's authority and search engine ranking.

= Is there a trial version of Adaptify SEO? =
Please visit our website or contact our support team for information on trial versions and pricing. We offer a 7 day free trial.
